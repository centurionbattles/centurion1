Piece Movement
  • Pawn: 1 step forward (2 from start); diagonal capture; promotes on the last rank.
  • Rook: any squares orthogonally (no jumping).
  • Knight: L-shape (jumps).
  • Bishop: any squares diagonally (no jumping).
  • Queen: rook + bishop moves.
  • King: 1 square in any direction.
  • Archer: exactly 2 squares in any direction; can jump.
  • Diplomat: moves like a King; can convert adjacent enemy non-King pieces.
